Privacy Policy – Group Feed

Effective Date: 2025-04-19

1. Information We Collect

When you use our app, we may collect the following data via the Facebook Graph API, only with your explicit permission:

  • Your name and Facebook ID

We do not collect private messages, emails, or any personal data beyond what Facebook provides through granted permissions.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

We do not sell or share your data. Access is read-only and strictly used to power the features of the application.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Your data is never shared with third parties. Any data retrieved is kept secure and used only by the user who authorized access.
